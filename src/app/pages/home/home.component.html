About Me
Dr. Sachin Subbaraya - Experienced General & Laparoscopic Surgeon

Dr. Sachin Subbaraya is an accomplished General and Laparoscopic Surgeon with over a decade of experience in advanced surgical procedures. After earning his MBBS and MS in General Surgery from the prestigious MS Ramaiah Institute, he began is career as a Senior Resident at Apollo Hospital. Currently, he serves as an Associate Consultant at Manipal Hospital where he specializes in minimally invasive surgical techniques.

His expertise spans a wide range of abdominal conditions, with a focus on laparoscopic gallbladder stone removal, appendectomy, hernia repair, and laser proctology for varicose veins and anorectal diseases. With a commitment to using cutting-edge technology, Dr. Sachin ensures minimal recovery time, reduced scarring, and superior surgical outcomes for his patients.
