Dr. Sachin Subbaraya Is An Accomplished General And Laparoscopic Surgeon With Over A Decade Of Experience In Advanced Surgical Procedures. After Earning His MBBS And MS In General Surgery From The Prestigious MS Ramaiah Institute, He Began His Career As A Senior Resident At Apollo Hospital. Currently, He Serves As An Associate Consultant At Manipal Hospital, Where He Specializes In Minimally Invasive Surgical Techniques.

His Expertise Spans A Wide Range Of Abdominal Conditions, With A Focus On Laparoscopic Gallbladder Stone Removal, Appendectomy, Hernia Repair, And Laser Proctology For Varicose Veins And Anorectal Diseases. With A Commitment To Using Cutting-Edge Technology, Dr. Sachin Ensures Minimal Recovery Time, Reduced Scarring, And Superior Surgical Outcomes For His Patients.
