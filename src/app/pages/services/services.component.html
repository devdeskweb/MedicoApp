Laparoscopic Surgeries
Gall Bladder Removal (Laparoscopic Cholecystectomy)
  • A Small Organ Under The Liver That Stores Bile.
  • Surgery Is Commonly Done To Remove Gallstones And Ease Pain.
  • Performed Using Keyhole Technique For Faster Recovery.
  • Types: Open Or Laparoscopic (Preferred For Minimal Pain).
Benefits:
  • Quick Recovery
  • No Large Cuts
  • Reduced Pain
  • No Recurrence Of Gallstones
Common Symptoms:
  • Abdominal Pain (Right Side)
  • Nausea, Vomiting
  • Bloating, Constipation, Or Diarrhea
Hernia Surgery
What is Hernia?
When An Organ Pushes Through A Weak Spot in Muscles. Common in Abdomen, Groin, Or Belly Area
Causes:
  • Heavy Lifting
  • Obesity
  • Constipation
  • Poor Nutrition
  • Previous Surgeries
Symptoms:
  • Groin Swelling
  • Pain When Lifting
  • Acid Reflux
  • Dull Ache
Why Laparoscopic Surgery?:
  • No Major Cuts Or Stitches
  • Painless & Faster Healing
  • Short Hospital Stay
Appendix Removal (Appendectomy)
What Is It?
Surgical Removal Of An Inflamed Appendix (Appendicitis). Can Be Done As:
  • Open Surgery (More Painful)
  • Laparoscopic Surgery (Minimally Invasive & Preferred)
Benefits Of Laparoscopic:
  • Minimal Pain
  • Quick Recovery
  • Small incisions
  • Less Risk Of Infection
Common Symptoms:
  • Abdominal Pain (Worse With Movement)
  • Bloating
  • Nausea & vomiting
  • Low-Grade Fever
Piles (Hemorrhoids) Treatment
What Are Piles?
Swollen Veins In The Rectum Or Anus. Can Be Internal Or External.
Symptoms:
  • Bleeding During Bowel Movements
  • Pain, Itching, Or Swelling In Rectal Area
  • Hard Lump In Anus
Risk Factors:
  • Constipation
  • Obesity
  • Pregnancy
  • Low-Fiber Diet
Treatment Options:
  • Home Remedies (Initial Stage)
  • Traditional Surgery (Painful)
  • Laser Treatment (Painless & Effective)
Why Choose Laser Treatment?:
  • No Cuts Or Stitches
  • Painless 30-45 Min Procedure
  • Discharge Same Day
  • Back To Normal in 48 Hours
