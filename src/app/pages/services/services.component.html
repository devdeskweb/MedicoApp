Hernia Surgery
What is Hernia?
When An Organ Pushes Through A Weak Spot in Muscles.
Common in Abdomen, Groin, Or Belly Area
Causes:
  • Heavy Lifting
  • Obesity
  • Constipation
  • Poor Nutrition
  • Previous Surgeries
Symptoms:
  • Groin Swelling
  • Pain When Lifting
  • Acid Reflux
  • Dull Ache
Why Laparoscopic Surgery?:
  • No Major Cuts Or Stitches
  • Painless & Faster Healing
  • Short Hospital Stay

Piles (Hemorrhoids) Treatment
What Are Piles?
Swollen Veins In The Rectum Or Anus. Can Be Internal Or External.
Symptoms:
  • Bleeding During Bowel Movements
  • Pain, Itching, Or Swelling In Rectal Area
  • Hard Lump In Anus
Risk Factors:
  • Constipation
  • Obesity
  • Pregnancy
  • Low-Fiber Diet
Treatment Options:
  • Home Remedies (Initial Stage)
  • Traditional Surgery (Painful)
  • Laser Treatment (Painless & Effective)
Why Choose Laser Treatment?:
  • No Cuts Or Stitches
  • Painless 30-45 Min Procedure
  • Discharge Same Day
  • Back To Normal in 48 Hours
